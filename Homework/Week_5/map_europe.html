The world map dataset consists of the top 1000 billionaires worldwide. The colouring on the map shows the Gross Domestic Product (GDP) of the countries. When you hover over a country, the number of billionaires (from the top 1000) in that country are shown, along with the number of billionaires in different age groups. The map and hover option are useful together, because you can easily see whether countries with a high GDP have more billionaires. Furthermore, a user can change the colour theme of the map either to blue or green.
